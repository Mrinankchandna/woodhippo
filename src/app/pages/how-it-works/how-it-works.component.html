Frequently Asked Questions

How does WOODHIPPO verify service providers?

We conduct thorough background checks, verify business credentials, check references, and review past work history to ensure all providers meet our quality standards.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with a service?

We have a satisfaction guarantee policy. If you're not satisfied with the service provided, contact our support team within 48 hours and we'll work to resolve the issue.

How are payments handled?

Payments are processed securely through our platform. Funds are held in escrow until the service is completed satisfactorily, protecting both clients and providers.

Can I hire providers for recurring services?

Yes, you can set up recurring service schedules (daily, weekly, monthly) with your preferred service providers through our platform.

What areas do you serve?

We currently operate exclusively in Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh, focusing on providing high-quality local services to businesses in our community.

How much does it cost to use WOODHIPPO?

For businesses, using our platform to find and hire service providers is free. We charge a small service fee to providers for jobs secured through our platform.
